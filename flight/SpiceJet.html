About SpiceJet

Airline Summary

Budget carrier SpiceJet (SG) is India’s second-biggest airline when measured by the number of domestic passengers. The airline operates a fleet of Boeing 737s, Dash 8- Q400 & freighters and is the country’s largest regional player operating 63 daily flights under UDAN or the Regional Connectivity Scheme. The airline flies to over 60 destinations, including more than 50 within India. The airline’s international destinations include Bangladesh, Oman, Sri Lanka, Thailand and the United Arab Emirates. The majority of the airline’s fleet offers SpiceMax, the most spacious economy class seating in India. Passengers who opt for SpiceMAX receive priority check-in, boarding & baggage delivery; seats with extra legroom; and complimentary meal & beverage. SpiceJet offers in-flight entertainment on-board through its path-breaking SpiceScreen system, wherein flyers can stream a wide variety of content directly on their personal devices. The airline also operates a dedicated air cargo service under the brand name SpiceXpress offering safe, on-time, efficient and seamless cargo connectivity across India and on international routes.
