About Jet Airways [no longer operating]

Airline Summary

NOTE: Operations suspended 17 April 2019 Based in Mumbai, Jet Airways (9W) is the second largest airline in India. It flies non-stop to more than 45 domestic destinations and more than 20 international destinations in 19 countries in Asia, Europe and North America. The airline's primary hub is located at Mumbai's Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport (BOM). Secondary hubs can be found at Chennai International Airport (MAA), Delhi's Indira Gandhi International Airport (DEL) and Kolkata's Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose International Airport (CCU). The airline's fleet includes aircraft with one-cabin (Economy Class), two-cabin (Première Class, or Business Class, and Economy Class) and three-cabin (First Class, Première Class and Economy Class) configurations.
